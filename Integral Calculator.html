How to write integrals:

  • intx^2dx or ∫x^2dx → ∫ x² dx
  • int2/xdx or ∫ 2/x dx → ∫ 2/x dx
  • int int x*y dx dy → double integral (iterated, no bounds)
  • Use the checkbox + bounds for definite integrals instead of typing them directly.
  • Bounds can be inf, -inf, ∞, or -∞ for improper integrals.
